Hello World! My name is Joseph Caswell. I grew up in Tallahassee, FL. Not the biggest city in Florida, but it is the capital. Despite being the worlds biggest FSU fan, my interest in math and science lead me to Purdue University where I majored in Civil Engineering. Purdue was definitely the hardest experience of my life. The rigor of the program challenged me and my peers constantly. Outside of that, there was always fun things to do and people to meet. I honestly miss just walking in the snow to the gym for a late night workout, or playing some basketball with my friends. After graduating, I decided to pursue another degree in Computer Science at Oregon State University. I found that coding was my true passion. I've fully immersed myself into the field by leaving traditional engineering and becoming a Teaching Assistant for Oregon State and a Software Intern for Walmart. I'm excited to keep advancing my skills everyday and challenging myself!
